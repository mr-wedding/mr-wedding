The Story of Us

Story of Us

Dating websites aren't all bad!

  • The beginning...

    24th May 2016

    We first met at the University of Kent, where River worked at the cafe and Maddie was a student. While most couples would meet in a bar or club, we found each other on a dating site, which may (or may not) rhyme with Cinder. After talking for a few hours and meeting the very next day, we haven’t looked back since.

  • The distance...

    16th June 2016

    Shortly into our relationship, Maddie’s work sent her across the globe to America, for three months! It was difficult for us both spending our first summer together so far apart. It became our routine to talk on the phone every day, and with the time difference either Maddie called at 5 AM her time, or River called at 10 PM his time. There were struggles, but we never stopped counting the days until Maddie came back. Seeing each other at the airport made it worth the wait.

    Thankfully the following holidays were spent together, including Paris at New Years, which has become our favourite place.

  • The deal...

    2019

    As the years passed, we settled into a delightful flat in Medway, a place that was entirely our own, but Maddie had grown ‘antsy’ for marriage. So a deal was struck! The condition, she had to stop asking and thus spoiling every surprise idea he had. In return, River promised that he would propose to her by the end of March 2020.

  • The evening...

    2020

    During an evening with friends in January, all the pieces of River’s plan were in place, and the dice began to roll. They gathered to indulge in a shared love of tabletop games, and while they distracted Maddie, River prepared a dazzling ring and a handwritten note.

  • The proposal...

    11th January 2020

    As their game drew to a close, River bestowed a gift to each player. Whilst Maddie read her handwritten note, River lowered onto one knee and proposed. Once the tears of joy had subsided and River apologised for surprising her, she said yes!

  • The planning...

    2020-2022

    Since the proposal, the wedding planning has been an incredible rollercoaster! Lockdown has complicated a lot of planning, and we are both beginning new jobs and courses, so balancing everything has been tough. But with the wedding coming closer and closer, as well as slowly coming together, we couldn’t be more excited!
